About the Event

Toyota & Lexus.
Led by the AE86.

86 Fest is Ireland's home for Toyota and Lexus — a festival built around the legendary AE86, alongside every other Toyota and Lexus worth bringing out. Show cars, on-track action, and the community that keeps the badge alive.

  • Show & Shine

    The cleanest, loudest, and most-built Toyotas and Lexus in the country — AE86s up front — all in one paddock.

  • On-Track Action

    Drivers running real laps on a real circuit. No barriers between the cars and the crowd.

  • The Community

    Hosted by JDM Classics and the people that turn up. A meet first, an event second.

Headline Guests

Drifting Royalty.
Coming to Ireland.

86 Fest 26 is bringing drifting royalty to Ireland. Legendary Japanese drift duo Shinji & Masayo Minowa will be joining us on 6th September for what is shaping up to be our biggest event yet — D1GP history, wild builds, and a driving style Japan is famous for, up close in Mondello.

Headline Guest

Shinji & Masayo Minowa

D1GP legends · Japan

From iconic D1GP history to some of the wildest cars and driving style Japan has ever produced, the Minowas landing in Mondello is a huge moment for the Irish drift scene. Come meet them, see the cars up close, and be part of the madness.
